Self-Introduction

Hello! I'm Yunji, and I'm a student in the Department of Information and Communication Engineering at Dongguk University.

I like robots and I want to study communication for moving it. So, I took classes in robotics and enjoyed studying. I also joined a club to make self-driving cars(MACARON5.0) and studied path planning as part of the team. It was a great opportunity for me.

I have the advantage of enjoying every day. However, because of too much enjoyment, I neglected to review what I learned in the past. So, I started blogging, which became enjoyable as I documented what I studied and my daily life. Please visit my Tistory.

I hope to become happier and have no regrets in the future.

Details of my past and ongoing projects can be found at 'Projects' session. I have documented my research and study, including team collaborations.

Please feel free to contact me if you are interested in my research and would like to contact me.

Areas of Interest

ITS, V2X, UAV

My primary area of interest is Smart City development, with a specific focus on mobility solutions using AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things). In other words, you can think of me being interested in ITS(Intelligent Transportation Systems), V2X(Vehicle-to-Everything), or UAV(Unmanned Aerial Vehicle). I think this requires a thorough understanding of communication, and I am passionate about developing this expertise.

In the field of unmanned mobility, I think that awareness of the surrounding environment and communication with surrounding devices are of paramount importance. And I think that if the network infrastructure is sufficiently equipped, it can be developed sufficiently through network communication. 

So, I would like to study the communication that I will use in the smart city field.

And to that end, I would like to study more about the various technologies currently being used.

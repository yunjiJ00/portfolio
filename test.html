Areas of Interest

ITS, V2X, UAV

My primary area of interest is Smart City development, with a specific focus on mobility solutions using AIoT (Artificial Intelligence of Things). In other words, you can think of me being interested in ITS(Intelligent Transportation Systems), V2X(Vehicle-to-Everything), or UAV(Unmanned Aerial Vehicle). I think this requires a thorough understanding of communication, and I am passionate about developing this expertise.

In the field of unmanned mobility, I think that awareness of the surrounding environment and communication with surrounding devices are of paramount importance. And I think that if the network infrastructure is sufficiently equipped, it can be developed sufficiently through network communication. 

So, I would like to study the communication that I will use in the smart city field.

And to that end, I would like to study more about the various technologies currently being used.
